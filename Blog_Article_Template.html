Solution & Approach

1

Data Architecture & Integration

Connected Power BI directly to SAP HANA tables containing cost center actuals, budget data, and production volumes. Built a data model with proper relationships between fact tables (costs, production) and dimension tables (time, cost centers, facilities). Implemented incremental refresh to handle historical data efficiently.

2

DAX Measure Development

Created complex DAX measures for operating cost per barrel calculations, year-over-year variance analysis, and budget variance tracking. Implemented time intelligence functions to enable flexible period comparisons (MTD, QTD, YTD) and rolling averages for trend analysis.

3

Dashboard Design & User Experience

Designed an executive summary page showing overall cost performance with drill-through capabilities to detailed analysis by mining operations, upgrading units, and utilities. Incorporated conditional formatting to highlight variances exceeding thresholds and built interactive filters for ad-hoc analysis.

4

Testing, Deployment & Training

Validated calculations against historical manual reports to ensure accuracy. Deployed to Power BI Service with scheduled refresh. Conducted training sessions with Finance team on dashboard usage and interpretation. Established feedback loop for continuous improvement.

Results & Outcomes

The automated dashboard transformed how Oil Sands Finance conducts monthly stewardship reporting, delivering measurable improvements in efficiency, accuracy, and insight generation.

Quantitative Results

  • Reduced monthly reporting time from 4 hours to 30 minutes (87.5% reduction)
  • Eliminated 2-3 calculation errors per quarter that previously required rework
  • Enabled analysis of $500M+ in annual operating costs across 12 facilities
  • Identified $2.3M in cost optimization opportunities in first 6 months
  • Reduced time-to-insight from 3-5 days to less than 24 hours post month-end

Qualitative Impact

  • Finance team freed up to focus on value-added analysis rather than data compilation
  • Leadership gained confidence in real-time cost visibility and trend analysis
  • Cross-functional collaboration improved through shared data platform
  • Established foundation for additional automated reporting initiatives
  • Dashboard adopted as template for other business units within Suncor

Lessons Learned

Data Quality is Critical: Initial implementation revealed data quality issues in SAP cost center mappings that required cleanup before accurate reporting was possible.

User Adoption Requires Training: Even intuitive dashboards require proper training and documentation. Created quick-reference guides and hosted office hours to support adoption.

Incremental Rollout Works Best: Started with executive summary, validated with stakeholders, then added drill-down functionality based on feedback rather than building everything upfront.
